Vision

To create a platform where people can interact with intelligent avatars of influential figures — thinkers, scientists, inventors, authors, and creators — making their knowledge and voices accessible to everyone, across generations and levels of expertise.

Mission

Scriptoverse builds AI‑trained avatars based on the life and work of real individuals. These avatars adapt to the user’s level of understanding, bring knowledge to life through dialogue, and preserve voices and works for future generations.

We begin with professors, scientists, researchers, inventors, poets, and authors — building a library of avatars that democratizes access to human knowledge and creativity.
